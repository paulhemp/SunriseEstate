Great Food & Local Favourites

Our hand-picked cafés and restaurants across the Byron Hinterland — from casual local favourites to destination dining.

The Byron Hinterland is a genuinely special food region. These are the places we send friends and family to — a mix of hearty local cafés, excellent Asian food, fine dining on the water, and a few hidden hinterland gems you'd only find with a tip-off.

Mullumbimby

7 minutes' drive from Sunrise Estate. The creative heart of the hinterland — and packed with good food.

The Paddock Project

Fresh, local produce and innovative dishes — a true farm-to-table experience. Perfect for seasonal, creative cuisine.

📍 64 Argyle Street, Mullumbimby

Izakaya Yu

Authentic Japanese — cosy atmosphere, excellent sushi, sashimi and traditional dishes. A must-visit for Japanese food lovers.

📍 53 Stuart Street, Mullumbimby

Pink Lotus

Our pick for Vietnamese — fresh, flavourful, warmly served. Easy lunch or dinner in a relaxed setting.

📍 Mullumbimby

Spice It Up Thai

Authentic Thai flavours with a wide range of classics. Perfect for a flavourful evening out.

📍 Mullumbimby

Rock 'n' Roll Café

A vibrant, retro-vibed café serving hearty breakfasts, lunches and excellent coffee. A local favourite.

📍 Mullumbimby

Santos Health Food

An institution for organic groceries, wholefoods and a much-loved café. Perfect for picnic supplies, smoothies and healthy breakfasts.

📍 Mullumbimby

While you're in Mullum — two excellent spas

Banye Spa and Kiva Spa are both nearby and make a lovely complement to a long lunch. Bathhouses, saunas, massages — easy to turn an afternoon into a full reset.

Brunswick Heads

10–20 minutes' drive. A charming riverside village with great cafés and a genuinely special restaurant or two.

Fleet Restaurant

A renowned fine-dining experience — fresh seasonal seafood, an exquisite wine list, and a setting to match. Book well ahead.

📍 Shop 2, 16 The Terrace, Brunswick Heads

Roco Ramen

Comforting, authentic Japanese ramen with rich broths and fresh ingredients. A warming, satisfying meal.

📍 Brunswick Heads

Brunswick Heads Health Foods Café

Healthy, organic meals, juices and smoothies. A good stop for wholesome dining before or after the beach.

📍 Brunswick Heads

Byron Bay

20 minutes' drive. Don't miss The Farm — it really is wonderful, with plenty for everyone.

Three Blue Ducks at The Farm

Rustic yet refined, with a strong emphasis on sustainable, locally sourced produce. The Farm itself is a destination in its own right — produce store, bakery, animals, playground.

📍 The Farm, Byron Bay

Raes on Wategos

An iconic Byron establishment — Mediterranean-inspired cuisine with stunning ocean views. A luxurious dining experience.

📍 Wategos Beach, Byron Bay

The Fig Tree Restaurant

Beautiful setting and a modern Australian menu focused on local ingredients. Ideal for a special occasion with stunning views.

📍 Byron Bay

Forest Byron Bay

A sophisticated dining experience with contemporary Australian cuisine and a relaxed ambiance. Perfect for a refined evening.

📍 Byron Bay

Loft Byron Bay

Stylish venue offering delicious food, cocktails and a great atmosphere — a night out with friends or a special date.

📍 Byron Bay

Federal & the Hinterland Hills

15 minutes' drive up into the hills.

Doma Café

Excellent Japanese tucked away in a hinterland village. Beautiful setting, thoughtful food — worth the scenic drive and our pick for a relaxed weekday lunch.

📍 Federal, NSW

Dining Tips

Reservations: For Fleet Restaurant and Raes on Wategos, book well in advance, especially during peak seasons.

Local favourites: Mullumbimby's casual cafés and health-food spots are where locals eat — excellent coffee and fresh, organic options.

Transport: We can help arrange transport to nearby restaurants. Just ask when you enquire.

Dietary requirements: Most restaurants in the area happily accommodate dietary preferences and allergies — mention your needs when you book.
