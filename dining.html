Great Food Nearby

Discover the culinary delights of the Byron Hinterland, from casual cafes to fine dining experiences.

The Byron Hinterland is a culinary delight, offering a diverse range of dining experiences from casual cafes to fine dining. Whether you're craving fresh local produce, authentic international cuisine, or innovative fusion dishes, you'll find something to satisfy every palate. Here are our top recommendations in Mullumbimby, Brunswick Heads, and Byron Bay.

Mullumbimby

The Paddock Project

Known for its fresh, local produce and innovative dishes, The Paddock Project offers a true farm-to-table experience. Perfect for those seeking seasonal, creative cuisine.

📍 64 Argyle Street, Mullumbimby

Izakaya Yu

A local favorite for authentic Japanese cuisine, offering a cozy atmosphere and delicious sushi, sashimi, and other traditional dishes. A must-visit for Japanese food lovers.

📍 53 Stuart Street, Mullumbimby

Pink Lotus

A popular spot for Vietnamese food, offering fresh and flavorful dishes in a relaxed setting. Enjoy authentic flavors and warm hospitality.

📍 Mullumbimby

Spice It Up Thai

For those craving authentic Thai flavors, this restaurant delivers with a wide range of classic dishes. Perfect for a flavorful evening out.

📍 Mullumbimby

Rock 'n' Roll Café

A vibrant cafe with a retro vibe, serving up hearty breakfasts, lunches, and great coffee. A local favorite for a casual, fun dining experience.

📍 Mullumbimby

Brunswick Heads

Fleet Restaurant

A renowned fine-dining experience focusing on fresh, seasonal seafood and an exquisite wine list. Perfect for a special occasion. Reservations are highly recommended.

📍 Shop 2, 16 The Terrace, Brunswick Heads

Roco Ramen

Perfect for a comforting bowl of authentic Japanese ramen, with rich broths and fresh ingredients. Ideal for a warming, satisfying meal.

📍 Brunswick Heads

Brunswick Heads Health Foods Cafe

A great option for healthy and organic meals, juices, and smoothies. Perfect for those seeking nutritious and wholesome dining options.

📍 Brunswick Heads

Byron Bay

The Fig Tree Restaurant

Offers a beautiful setting and a modern Australian menu with a focus on local ingredients. Ideal for a special occasion with stunning views.

📍 Byron Bay

Raes on Wategos

An iconic Byron Bay establishment offering Mediterranean-inspired cuisine with stunning ocean views. A luxurious dining experience not to be missed.

📍 Wategos Beach, Byron Bay

Three Blue Ducks, Byron Bay

Located at The Farm, this restaurant offers a rustic yet refined dining experience with a strong emphasis on sustainable and locally sourced produce.

📍 The Farm, Byron Bay

Doma Cafe (Federal)

While a short drive into the hinterland, Doma is famous for its exceptional Japanese food and charming village atmosphere. A must-visit for lunch.

📍 Federal, NSW

Forest Byron Bay

Offers a sophisticated dining experience with a focus on contemporary Australian cuisine and a relaxed ambiance. Perfect for a refined evening.

📍 Byron Bay

Loft Byron Bay

A stylish venue offering delicious food, cocktails, and a great atmosphere, perfect for a night out with friends or a special date.

📍 Byron Bay

Dining Tips

Reservations: For fine dining establishments like Fleet Restaurant and Raes on Wategos, we recommend booking in advance, especially during peak seasons.

Local Favorites: Don't miss the casual cafes and health food spots in Mullumbimby for a true local experience. Many offer excellent coffee and fresh, organic options.

Transport: We offer transport services to nearby restaurants. Speak with Paul about arranging a ride to your dining destination.

Dietary Requirements: Most restaurants in the area are happy to accommodate dietary preferences and allergies. Just mention your needs when booking.
