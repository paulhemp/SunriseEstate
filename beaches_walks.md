# Beaches & Walks: Explore the Natural Beauty of the Byron Hinterland

The Byron Hinterland and its surrounding coastline offer an abundance of natural beauty, from pristine beaches to lush rainforest walks and spectacular waterfalls. Here’s a guide to some of the best spots to explore during your stay at Sunrise Estate.

## Beaches

### Byron Bay Beaches

*   **Main Beach**: The central hub of Byron Bay, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and people-watching. Easily accessible with plenty of facilities.
*   **Clarkes Beach**: A popular spot for families and beginner surfers, offering calmer waters than Main Beach and beautiful views of the lighthouse.
*   **Wategos Beach**: A picturesque, sheltered cove ideal for swimming, stand-up paddleboarding, and longboarding. Known for its stunning scenery and relaxed atmosphere.
*   **Little Wategos**: The easternmost beach in Australia, offering a secluded and tranquil experience. Accessible via a short walk from Wategos.
*   **Tallow Beach**: A long, expansive stretch of beach south of the lighthouse, popular for surfing, fishing, and peaceful walks away from the crowds.
*   **Broken Head Beach**: A beautiful, less crowded beach with excellent surfing conditions and scenic headland walks.

### Brunswick Heads Beaches

*   **Torakina Beach**: A sheltered, picturesque beach at the mouth of the Brunswick River, perfect for families with young children due to its calm waters. It’s a hidden gem just a short walk from Brunswick’s charming cafes.
*   **Main Beach (Brunswick Heads)**: Offers long stretches of sand ideal for walking, swimming, and enjoying the relaxed coastal vibe of Brunswick Heads.

### Other Notable Beaches

*   **Belongil Beach**: Stretching north from Byron’s Main Beach, Belongil is known for its dog-friendly sections and relaxed atmosphere.
*   **Kings Beach**: A more secluded option, offering tranquility and natural beauty.
*   **Lennox Head**: A famous point break for surfers, with a stunning headland walk offering panoramic views.

## Walks & National Parks

### Goonengerry National Park

Just a 15-minute drive from Sunrise Estate, Goonengerry National Park is a hidden gem offering beautiful rainforest walks and stunning waterfalls. 

*   **Goonengerry Waterfalls**: An easy walk to the waterfalls, which can be done as a loop. Enjoy the lush rainforest, lookouts, and opportunities for swimming.
*   **South Boundary Loop**: A 1.5 – 2 hour walk through shaded tracks, with a deviation to a beautiful waterfall.

### Minyon Falls (Nightcap National Park)

Approximately 20 minutes from Sunrise Estate, Minyon Falls is a spectacular natural wonder within Nightcap National Park. 

*   **Minyon Falls Walking Track**: Offers a range of forest experiences, pristine creeks, and breathtaking views of the waterfall. You can walk to the base of the falls for a refreshing swim.
*   **Minyon Grass Trail**: The quickest way to reach the base of Minyon Falls, just over 2km each way with less elevation.

### Other Walks

*   **Byron Bay Lighthouse Walk**: An iconic walk offering panoramic views of the coastline, perfect for spotting dolphins and whales during migration season.
*   **Rainforest Hikes**: The entire Byron Hinterland is dotted with numerous trails through lush rainforests, offering opportunities to discover hidden gems and local wildlife. Jarien, our qualified Nature Guide, can lead private, tailored hikes.

Enjoy exploring the diverse landscapes of our beautiful region!
