# Google Sites Setup Guide for Sunrise Estate

Since Google Sites is a drag-and-drop builder, you can easily recreate the professional landing page I've designed by following these steps.

## 1. Initial Setup
- Go to [sites.google.com](https://sites.google.com) and start a new "Blank" site.
- **Site Name**: Sunrise Estate
- **Page Title**: Sunrise Estate

## 2. Hero Section (Header)
- **Header Type**: Large Banner or Cover.
- **Image**: Upload `hero_pool_house.webp`.
- **Text**: Add a text box over the image.
  - **Title**: Sunrise Estate (Font: Playfair Display or similar serif)
  - **Subtitle**: A Genuine Healing Retreat in the Heart of the Byron Hinterland.
- **Buttons**: Add two buttons:
  - **Book on Airbnb**: Link to `https://www.airbnb.com.au/rooms/7213250`
  - **WhatsApp Paul**: Link to `https://wa.me/61407767709`

## 3. Introduction Section
- Add a "Text Box" with centered alignment.
- **Copy**: "Wake to the sunrise over Cape Byron's lighthouse and watch the sun set into the valley from our lush gardens. Sunrise Estate is more than just a stay; it's a 22-acre sanctuary of rainforest and healing, shared with those who seek peace and rejuvenation."

## 4. Meet Your Host Section
- Use the "Content Block" with one image and one text area.
- **Image**: Upload `bedroom_view.webp`.
- **Text**: 
  - **Heading**: Meet Your Host: Paul
  - **Body**: "Paul brings over 30 years of expertise in wellness, hemp, and plant medicine. As a meditation teacher, qualified masseuse, and author of 18 books, he has curated Sunrise Estate to be a place of genuine healing and profound experience."
  - **Quote**: "Healing at Sunrise Estate is genuine — not just aesthetic."

## 5. Offerings & Experiences (Grid)
- Use the "Content Block" with 3 images/text or 4 images/text.
- For each offering (Sound Bed, Tea Ceremony, Massage, etc.), add a small icon or image and the corresponding text from the `content.md` file.
- **Tip**: Use the "Sound Bed" as the featured offering with a star ★.

## 6. Amenities Section
- Use a "Collapsible Group" or a simple "Text Box" with bullet points.
- **Amenities**: Chemical-Free Pool, Far-Infrared Sauna, Games Room, Orchard & Gardens, Sound Immersion.

## 7. Gallery Section
- Use the "Image Carousel" or "Grid" to upload:
  - `aerial_view.webp`
  - `dining_living_space.webp`
  - `sunrise_sunset_view.webp`
  - `chess_view.webp`

## 8. Nearby Section
- Add a "Text Box" with a 3-column layout for the nearby attractions and their distances.

## 9. Footer
- Add a "Footer" to the site.
- **Text**: "Ready to Rejuvenate? Contact Paul today."
- **Links**: WhatsApp: +61 407 767 709 | Airbnb: [Link]
- **Address**: 1107 Coolamon Scenic Drive, Montecollum NSW 2482

**Pro Tip**: You can also use the "Embed" feature in Google Sites and paste the code from `index.html` if you want to see how the custom design looks, though building it natively in Google Sites is better for mobile responsiveness and SEO.
