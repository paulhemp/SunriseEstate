A Glimpse of Paradise

Explore the Local Area

Sunrise Estate is ideally placed between the Byron hinterland's most beautiful waterfalls, beaches, villages, and foodie hotspots. Here's what's right on your doorstep.

Crystal Castle

Walking distance

One of Byron's best loved attractions — crystal gardens, a Buddha walk, cafés and an immersive landscape. Just a 1-minute drive or a pleasant stroll from the estate.

Goonengerry National Park

15 mins drive

A stunning waterfall just a 10-minute walk from the car park. Quiet rainforest tracks and swimmable pools — a local secret.

Minyon Falls

20 mins drive

One of the region's most spectacular waterfalls, with a range of hikes from short lookouts to a swimmable pool at the base.

Brunswick Heads

10–20 mins drive

Torakina Beach is protected and calm — ideal for families and gentle swims. The main beach is perfect for long, open walks. Charming cafés line the river.

Byron Bay & The Farm

20 mins drive

The Farm is wonderful — there's plenty for everyone, from Three Blue Ducks to the produce store, bakery and animals. Byron's lighthouse, beaches and main street are all close.

Mullumbimby

7 mins drive

The hinterland's creative heart. Highlights include Banye Spa and Kiva Spa, Pink Lotus (Vietnamese), Rock 'n' Roll Café, Santos Health Food, Spice It Up Thai, and much more.

Federal — Doma Café

15 mins drive

An excellent Japanese café tucked up in the hinterland hills. Beautiful setting, thoughtful food — well worth the scenic drive.

Nightcap National Park

20–30 mins drive

Ancient Gondwana rainforest with spectacular views, protected waterholes and walking tracks — part of UNESCO World Heritage.

Cape Byron Lighthouse

25 mins drive

The easternmost point of mainland Australia. Panoramic coastal walks, dolphin and whale spotting in season.
